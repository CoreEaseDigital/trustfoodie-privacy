Overview

About This Policy

This privacy policy applies to the TrustFoodie app for mobile devices, created by CoreEase Digital as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

By using TrustFoodie, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how we operate.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

The Application collects certain information when you use it. This includes:

IP Address
Your device's Internet Protocol address for network communication purposes.
Food Photos
Photos of food ingredient labels you capture in the app, transmitted securely to Google Gemini AI for analysis and deleted immediately after.
Dietary Preferences
Your selected dietary requirements stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

📍 The Application does not collect or track your precise geographic location at any time.

Artificial Intelligence

How We Use AI

TrustFoodie uses Google Gemini AI to power its core ingredient analysis feature. When you capture a photo of a food label, that image is securely transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing.

Ingredient Analysis
Photos are sent to Google Gemini to read and interpret ingredient lists, generating health grades, summaries, and dietary suitability assessments.
AI Chat Assistant
Follow-up questions about scanned products are processed through Google Gemini to provide informed, contextual responses.
Photo Handling
Photos are deleted immediately after analysis. They are never stored, shared with third parties, or used beyond generating your ingredient result.
⚠ AI Disclaimer

While Google Gemini AI is highly capable, it may occasionally misread text or misinterpret ingredients — particularly from blurry, angled, or poorly lit photos. TrustFoodie is an informational tool only and should not replace professional medical, dietary, or allergy advice. Always verify critical information directly from the product packaging.

Your Data

Data Retention and Your Rights

Your scan history and dietary preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. The Service Provider does not have access to this data.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app entirely.

To request deletion of any other data, contact us at coreeasedigital@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

Children's Privacy

TrustFoodie does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be immediately deleted.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at coreeasedigital@gmail.com.
