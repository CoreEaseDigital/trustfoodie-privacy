Overview

About This Policy

This privacy policy applies to the TrustFoodie app (referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, created by CoreEase Digital (referred to as the "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

By using TrustFoodie, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we operate.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

The Application collects certain information when you use it. This includes:

IP Address

Your device's Internet Protocol address for network communication purposes.

Food Photos

Photos of food ingredient labels you capture in the app, transmitted securely to Google Gemini AI for analysis and deleted immediately after.

Dietary Preferences

Your selected dietary requirements (e.g. Vegan, Gluten-Free, Halal) stored locally on your device only.

The Application does not collect or track your precise geographic location at any time.

Artificial Intelligence

How We Use AI

TrustFoodie uses Google Gemini AI to power its core ingredient analysis feature. When you capture a photo of a food label, that image is securely transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing.

Ingredient Analysis

Photos are sent to Google Gemini to read and interpret ingredient lists, generating grades, summaries, and dietary suitability assessments.

AI Chat Assistant

Follow-up questions about scanned products are processed through Google Gemini to provide informed, contextual responses.

Photo Handling

Photos are deleted immediately after analysis. They are never stored, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose beyond generating your ingredient result.

AI Disclaimer

While Google Gemini AI is highly capable, it may occasionally misread text or misinterpret ingredients — particularly from blurry, angled, or poorly lit photos. TrustFoodie is an informational tool only and should not replace professional medical, dietary, or allergy advice. Always verify critical ingredient information directly from the product packaging.

Third Parties

Third Party Services

TrustFoodie uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

The Service Provider may share information with third parties only as required by law, to protect rights and safety, or with trusted service providers who adhere to this privacy policy.

Advertising

Ads and Advertising ID

TrustFoodie is free to use and supported by advertising through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to serve relevant ads within the app.

Banner ads appear at the top of the screen, and occasional full-screen interstitial ads may appear between scans. You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device's settings under Privacy then Ads.

Your Data

Data Retention and Your Rights

Your scan history and dietary preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. The Service Provider does not have access to this data.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app entirely.

To request deletion of any other data held by the Service Provider, contact us at coreeasedigital@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

Children's Privacy

TrustFoodie does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be immediately deleted.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at coreeasedigital@gmail.com.

Opt Out

Stopping Data Collection

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall process available on your mobile device or via the Google Play Store.
